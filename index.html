About Me

I am a Senior Front-End Web Developer with more than 13 years of experience, almost exclusively in the financial services advertising space. I pick up new programming languages quickly, and take pride in writing clean, well-organized code. Along with a wealth of experience with Javascript, HTML, and CSS development, I am proficient in many Adobe Suite programs and other design tools, such as Figma. I have experience with Vue and React and many of the libraries associated with those languages, such as NodeJS, Vuetify, Vuex, and Remotion. I am currently working on a certification in Full-Stack Web Development through Codecademy.
